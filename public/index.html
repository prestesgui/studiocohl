About

I’m a Motion Designer and Video Editor with over 16 years of experience across animation, advertising, post-production and branded content.

I’ve collaborated with global brands including PlayStation, Vodafone, Heinz and KPMG, producing motion design that blends storytelling, rhythm and visual identity across digital and broadcast media.

I also contributed to the animated feature *Light of the World*, directed by Tom Bancroft (Disney: The Lion King, Mulan, Beauty and the Beast).
