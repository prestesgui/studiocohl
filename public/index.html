About

I’m a Motion Designer and Video Editor with over 16 years of experience across animation, advertising, post-production and branded content.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated with agencies, studios and clients across Europe, North America and international markets, creating visual content that combines design, rhythm and storytelling across digital and broadcast media.

My portfolio includes work for globally recognised brands such as PlayStation, Vodafone, Heinz and KPMG, delivering motion graphics, commercial campaigns and engaging visual experiences for diverse audiences.

I also had the opportunity to contribute to the animated feature film Light of the World, directed by Tom Bancroft, former Disney animator known for his work on The Lion King, Mulan and Beauty and the Beast.

Today I help brands, agencies and creative teams transform ideas into impactful visual communication through motion design, editing and creative direction.
